Warren College Student Council

Warren College Student Council (WCSC) represents the interests and opinions of Warren College Students on college, university, and related matters. We provide representatives to campus-wide committees, provide for Warren College Student Organizations, and have an Events Board to coordinate programs that benefit our Warren community.
